The Climate Change

Climate change refers to significant, long-term changes in the Earth's climate, particularly an increase in temperature.This phenomenon is largely driven by human activities, such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrialprocesses, which release large amounts of greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide (CO2) and methane (CH4) into theatmosphere.
